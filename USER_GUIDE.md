# LMS Training Automation - User Guide

## Quick Start

1. **Open**: https://video-tutor-4.preview.emergentagent.com
2. **Login**: Enter your YourHippo credentials
3. **Click**: "Fetch Courses" 
4. **Start**: Click "Quick Start" or select a specific course
5. **Monitor**: Watch real-time progress in Activity Log
6. **Complete**: When you see 🏆 "Final quiz ready", take the quiz yourself

## Features

✅ Automatic video playback  
✅ Auto-solve in-video quizzes (unlimited attempts)  
✅ Real-time progress tracking  
✅ Stops before final quiz  
✅ Prioritizes overdue courses  

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Credentials Required**: You must provide valid YourHippo LMS login credentials  
⚠️ **Final Quiz Manual**: The tool stops before final quizzes - you must take them yourself  
⚠️ **One at a Time**: Processes courses sequentially, not in parallel  
⚠️ **Network Required**: Requires stable internet connection  

## Need Help?

**Login Issues**: Verify your credentials work at https://lms.yourhippo.com/ first  
**Automation Stuck**: Click the "Stop" button and try again  
**No Courses**: Click "Refresh" or check your LMS account
