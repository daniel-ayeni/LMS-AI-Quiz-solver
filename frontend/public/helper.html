❓ FAQ

Q: Will this watch the videos for me?
A: No - videos must play naturally in your real browser. Script only automates the quiz answering.
Q: Can I close the tab?
A: No, keep it open (you can minimize though).
Q: Will it take the final quiz?
A: No - only in-video quizzes. You take the final quiz yourself.
Q: Is this safe?
A: Yes! It runs entirely in your browser, no data sent anywhere.
